# Distribution Layer

## The What

This is where AI products reach users through channels, platforms, and marketplaces.

## Purpose

To scale the reach of AI applications by managing delivery, access, and adoption.

## Who Works Here

Growth engineers, Marketing teams, product managers.

## Focus Areas

- Delivery Channels
- Ecosystem Integration
- Strategic Partnerships
- Marketing & Awareness
- Compliance & Localization

## Output

- Market reach
- User adoption
- Revenue growth
